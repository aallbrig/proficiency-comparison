Explore US educational statistics with interactive charts. Year ranges and data points are automatically detected from available data.

Data Notes

Dynamic Data Display: Year ranges and data point counts are automatically detected from the available data files. Sections without data are not displayed.

  • Literacy: Adult literacy and high school completion rates from NCES Digest.
  • Attainment: Educational attainment levels (high school, associate's, bachelor's+, graduate) from US Census Bureau ACS.
  • Graduation: 4-year adjusted cohort graduation rates from NCES Digest.
  • Enrollment: School enrollment rates by age group from NCES Digest.
  • NAEP: Test proficiency scores require manual export from NAEP Data Explorer.
  • Early Childhood: Kindergarten readiness data from ECLS (requires restricted access).
